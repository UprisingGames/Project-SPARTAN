    About Project Spartan

    Project Spartan is the new project by Uprising Games. This is a 2 player battle game that will support local and eventually online multiplayer battling, with a customizable campain

    Epic Battles

    Battle online against people across the globe, or against your friends locally

    Epic Campaign

    Follow your soldier in his customizable campaign, with options for future, past, or present soldiers. Along with options for being good or evil and etc.
